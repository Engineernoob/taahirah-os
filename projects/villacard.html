🏡 VillaCard — AI Concierge & Booking Dashboard

VillaCard combines property management and travel AI. It offers villa owners and guests a unified dashboard to manage bookings, concierge requests, and financial insights powered by intelligent agents.

Stack: Next.js 15 · Supabase · TailwindCSS · Ollama (local LLM) · Radix UI · TypeScript

  • Credits-based booking system
  • AI concierge chat powered by local inference
  • Owner analytics and revenue dashboards
