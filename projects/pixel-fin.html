Pixel Fin

Instead of endless spreadsheets or boring graphs, you get: pixel coins 🪙, a 16-bit piggy bank 🐷, and a little virtual world that reacts to how you save or spend. Think Tamagotchi × budget tracker × Game Boy.

Stack: Expo SDK 51 · TailwindCSS · TypeScript · HuggingFace

  • 💰 Add Expense / Add Savings : Two big buttons to log spending or saving (manual input, no bank connection)
  • 🐷 Animated Pixel Pet : A piggy bank, cat, or frog that changes mood based on your balance.
  • 📊 Retro Dashboard : Shows daily balance as pixel bars, not charts.
  • 💾 Local Save : Uses AsyncStorage (mobile) or localStorage (web).
  • 🔔 “Lazy Alert”:Pet gives one-line commentary — e.g., “You saved again?! Unreal.”
