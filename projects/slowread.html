📖 Slow Read

Slow Read is a Calm Neurodivergent-Friendly Summary App, that has a local bookself. Think Notion × Blinkist × Stoic Reader.

Stack: Next.js 15 · TailwindCSS · Ollama (local LLM) · · TypeScript · HuggingFace

  • a Bookshelf feature that allows users to save, browse, and reread their books on the bookself.
  • AI-generated summaries in a beautiful, distraction-free interface.
